Services

Kimono

We have a selection of Kimonos from all over the country in all sizes. Our seasoned staff will guide you through this authentic Kimono costume experience.

Tea Ceremony

Matcha might sound more familiar to you now than it used to be, thanks to Matcha flavored drinks, but have you ever prepared Matcha in the traditional method? In this ceremony, you will be entertained with its peaceful process with freshly-foamed Matcha and Japanese sweet snacks called Wagashi (literal translation of Japanese snack).

Incense Ceremony

Incense ceremony was a game for aristcrats over 900 years ago. They not only “listened to” the fragrance as a form of appreciation, but also played guessing games over the subtle differences between types of incense wood.

Japanese Gift

In addition to a variety of Kimonos for sale for you to try and take home with, we have one of the largest selections of Japanese tradition gifts in the city. From the size of a matchbox to the size of your biggest suitcase, you will certainly find a perfect gift that will accompany you back to your home.
